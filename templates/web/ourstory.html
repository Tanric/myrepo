Founder Story

Well, I am “The Certified Engineer” which people discuss over a cup of hot tea, coming from a middle-class family, trying to fulfil my parent's dreams, frustrated from the monotony of work, routine tasks day in and day out, and seeing my fleeting ambitions, dreams, my passion diminishing and striving to get out of mediocrity,

It never happens overnight, unlike the general saying. It was the manifestation of a multitude of reasons over an extended timeline which resulted in me kicking myself up from the abyss, reigniting the lost fire, and bringing about a change, a change in myself, as well as my surroundings, and in turn the nation.

I was always an average percentile student, both in school, as well as during Graduation. But the love for making things, breaking things up, analysing them, and rebuilding it entirely encapsulating the core idea behind it always had my eye. It always helped me get a kinesthetic experience of how and why a concept works.

That was it, I geared up for spreading the same joy of making around, and reinventing the making experience. That is how Karkhana Makerspace was created, a place for makers, creators, innovators, artists, engineers, and hobbyists alike. It is now in the process of building traction across the Higher Education Sector with rapid strides and the vision of changing the way of education with help of hands-on learning.

Making India a progressive playground for the Makers Revolution is the goal I’m pursuing, and the dream I dream for each night.
