Karkhana Makerspace is a co-working and maker workshop. We are a co-curricular after-school and engineering program with a unique approach to learning and making. We are a team of teachers - engineers, designers, artists, and turn the classroom into a laboratory for discoveries and creations.
Karkhana Makerspace started in 2016 by Mr. Siddharth Bhatter (Microsoft Innovative Educator, Top 40 under 40 Young Innovative Entrepreneur, TedX Speaker, Guest Speaker, Guest Lecturer in leading academic institutions and finally an avid Electronics Enthusiast) started with a vision of changing the way of education with help of Hands-on learning.
Karkhana Makerspace provides the best combination of tools, equipment, instructors, access, and education for makers and startups for rapid prototyping. We offer hands-on STEM/skills training for students, employees, teachers, and everyone in our community.
