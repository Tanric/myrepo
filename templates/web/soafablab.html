Fablab Activities

Welcome to a community of people who have a passion for making things, and who want to share that with others by making at S’O’A Fab Lab, powered by Karkhana Makerspace. The greatest part about creating a Makerspace is that it has no rules. Libraries follow the same structure from city to city, where makerspace shape and grow to what the members of the organization and the community feel. Connecting the wise, experienced members of S’O’A University who have the field knowledge from Medical, Engineering, Film, and Design to the young, willing-to learn youth of the community.



Making and Makerspace are complementary to curriculum-driven classes. Both are necessary; one cannot have a surgeon or a pilot learning solely by making. Making involves what is often described as “the 4 C’s” of the necessary 21st century skills:
>Creative Thinking
>Critical Thinking
>Collaboration
>Communication
